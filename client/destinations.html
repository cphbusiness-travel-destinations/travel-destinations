Thailand

Khao Sok National Park

14 Feb, 2022 - 27 Feb, 2022

Khao Sok National Park in Thailand is renowned for its breathtaking limestone cliffs, lush rainforests, and the serene Cheow Lan Lake, offering visitors a unique blend of natural beauty and wildlife diversity. As one of Thailand's oldest rainforests, it provides exceptional opportunities for trekking, wildlife spotting, and boat tours amidst stunning landscapes.
